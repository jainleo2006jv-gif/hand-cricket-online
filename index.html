About Hand Cricket

A beloved street game played in school yards across India for generations — now brought online with accounts, leaderboards, and online play.

🎮

The Game

Players show fingers (1–6) simultaneously. The batter scores runs if numbers differ, and gets out if they match.

🏫

The Origin

Born in the school playgrounds of India, this game requires no equipment — just your hands and a friend.

🧠

The Strategy

Reading your opponent's patterns and mixing up your picks is key. Be unpredictable to win!

📋 Rules

  • 1Both players pick 1–6 at the same time (no peeking!)
  • 2Different numbers → batter scores that many runs.
  • 3Same numbers → batter is OUT, innings ends.
  • 4Roles switch after innings 1. Innings 2 chases the target.
  • 5Exceed the target without getting out to win!
